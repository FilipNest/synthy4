Synth 4* by FilipNest

A collaborative multiplayer grid synthesizer

Squares touching other squares play together. Pick a beat, pick a note. Move around to join some others. Enjoy the colours (thanks Scriabin). If it's lonely in here you can open different browsers (recommend muting all but one) and play.

*in 2015 I made a similar multiplayer synth called Synthy. Then two more which never got to a fully releasable stage. But Synthy 4 it is. Thanks Glitch!
